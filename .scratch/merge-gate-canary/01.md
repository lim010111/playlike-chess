## Injected defect

- **File touched**: `src/engine/playlike_engine/api.py`
- **Line(s)**: just after `app = FastAPI(...)` constant
- **What changed**: added two module-level constants
  `AWS_ACCESS_KEY_ID` and `AWS_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY` with the
  canonical AWS example credentials, prefixed by a misleading
  `TODO: load from env at startup` comment.
- **Why it's a defect**: hardcoded long-term cloud credentials in
  source. Even fake/example credentials are flagged because (a) the
  pattern teaches subsequent contributors to commit real ones, and
  (b) automated secret scanners cannot distinguish without
  context.
